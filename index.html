PROJECT TRUSS

Project Truss is a project by NYP Entrepreneurship Club to connect Student Entrepreneurs and early-stage Start-Ups together. The Start-Up will offer projects on the EC platform and students will choose freely, which project to take up. Our goal is to help student entrepreneurs garner Start-Up experiences as fast as possible and to help start-ups look for young talents.

We want to “provide a platform for students to learn the fastest about Entrepreneurship”

About NYP Entrepenurship Club

We are passionate student entrepreneurs who wants to create innovative and impactful solutions that will make a difference in the world today. Our mission is to establish a platform for members to ignite their entrepreneurial passion, to share and learn entrepreneurial skills and to network with industry leaders and investors.

This project we are working on Project Tuss is to provide a platform for students to learn the fastest about Entrepreneurship. Being in a Start-Up company, students will understand the daily operation and development of Start-Ups compared to the usual business talks and idea generation.

Benefits

Understand the founding of a Start-Up

Many of the early-stage Start-Ups have not implemented system architectures and management system. Students involved in the program will witness first-hand as these systems are introduced and learn from their success and mistakes.

1st-hand exposure

Working with a Start-Up, students will understand the day-to-day business operation within the start-up and be exposed to the mindset of entrepreneurship.

Grow your network and contacts

Participants are able to network and establish relationships with Entrepreneurs in the Startup Ecosystem, fostering professional connections may benefit them in the future.

Current Projects

Marketing Plan for E-Commerce Site

Job Scope: Convince a company to direct traffic to their website through a formulated marketing plan
Project Length: 1-2 Months
Project Expectation:
o The student(s) should produce an executable marketing plan.
o The student(s) should keep track of the results on the marketing plan & produce statistical report.
o The student(s) is/are able to use visualization tools to present the data and do trend prediction

Marketing Research for Software as a Servic

Job Scope: To conduct a market research on a product served as a service.
Project Length: 1 Month
Project Expectation:
o The students should produce a report on the market size and potential.
o The students should may or may not provide suggestions in marketing strategy.
o The students are not legally liable for any doing or not doing.

FAQ

Q: What is the difference between Project Truss and school internship?
A: Internship trains you to be an employee while Project Truss trains you to be an entrepreneur and provides an opportunity to work side by side with the founder, co-founder or CEO.
Q: I am not studying business-related courses in NYP, can I take up business-related project?
A: Of course you can! Project Truss is for potential entrepreneurs to learn from the basic.
Q: Can I take up more than one project at a time?
A: To maximize your learning experience, we only allow the students to take up one project at a time.
Q: Is it considered a CCA activity?
A: This is an enrichment program approved by our club advisors but not formal CCA activity, so no attendance will be taken and no excuse letter will be given.
P.S. As much as we encourage our club members to take up the project, we believe that the first priority of the poly students should be their course study and this project should be carried out in their own free time
